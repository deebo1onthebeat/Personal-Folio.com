Relevance

The profession of Web-developer is actual for me, as in the future I want to become a professional Web-developer. This project is important for me because it allows me to confirm or refute my choice of individual educational trajectory

Attending classes of computer science in basic school and the elective course "Web-design and site-building basics" I liked the theme "Introduction to Programming". I wondered if I could become a programmer, namely Web-programmer, create Web-sites. I decided to try myself as a Web-site developer, in order to confirm or deny my choice of IOT

Hypothesis

I assume that I made the right choice of IOT and can become a professional Web-developer

Target

Confirmation of the correctness of the choice of IOT, through the development of Web - site and placing it on the Internet>

Task

  • Study of professional duties and personal qualities of the Web-developer
  • Learn about site-building technologies
  • Familiarize yourself with the main programming languages and start learning them
  • Choose a platform for website development
  • Develop site layout
  • Conduct testing and debugging of the site
  • Publish the site on the Internet
  • Conclusions

Resuit

Confirmation or disproof of the hypothesis>
